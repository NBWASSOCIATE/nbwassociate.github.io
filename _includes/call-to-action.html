Company Profile

NBW & CO – Chartered Accountants & Business Consultants
NBW & CO is a reputable chartered accounting and professional business consultancy firm headquartered in Malaysia. The firm is led by Mr. Ng Boh Wah, a distinguished Chartered Accountant and seasoned business advisor, whose leadership has been instrumental in establishing NBW & CO as a trusted partner for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).

With a strong emphasis on financial precision, regulatory compliance, and strategic business advisory, NBW & CO is committed to delivering tailored, end-to-end professional services. These include:

  • Accounting & Financial Reporting
  • Tax Planning & Compliance
  • Corporate Secretarial Services
  • Business Consultancy & Strategic Advisory

Our Founder: Mr. Ng Boh Wah
Mr. Ng Boh Wah, the founding partner of NBW & CO, brings deep expertise and a strong professional standing in the financial and corporate services sector. His credentials include:

  • Chartered Accountant, Malaysian Institute of Accountants (MIA)
  • Fellow Member, Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA), UK
  • ASEAN Chartered Professional Accountant (ACPA)
  • Licensed Company Secretary – authorized to provide statutory secretarial services including company formation, compliance filings, and governance advisory

Educated at Sunway University, Mr. Ng is recognized for his practical insights and commitment to upholding the highest standards of ethics, professionalism, and service excellence.
